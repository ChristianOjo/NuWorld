Our Story

More Than a
Media House.

NuWorld Media is a collaborative effort between three specialist agencies — Triple Three Media, Dotmedia and Nkente Media — whose combined expertise spans Eswatini, South Africa, Lesotho and Mozambique.

Together, we form a symphony that produces powerful works in the media space. The combination of our individual strengths and years of expertise across different markets renders us an invincible dream team in transit advertising.

"The combination of strengths and years of expertise in different countries render us an invincible dream team in the media space."

We offer innovative platforms that guarantee our clients' campaigns reach their target audience effectively. We cover a wide scope of marketing works with a particular focus on transit advertising — and we have significant reach in Eswatini.
