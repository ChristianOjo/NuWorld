Platform 01

Transit
Advertising.

Mobile billboard advertising is one of the most powerful and cost-effective mediums available. By transforming buses and kombis into dynamic, attention-grabbing rolling billboards, we guarantee massive brand impressions across every city, town and village in Eswatini.

Our kombis function as three mobile billboards for the price of one — with both side panels branded for maximum visibility in both directions, plus rear branding that captures every trailing vehicle on highways and national roads.

Platform 02

Commuter
TV.

Our Commuter TV platform places permanently installed screens inside kombi taxi fleets across Eswatini. These screens run all day, every day as the taxi makes its routes — delivering your video advertisement to a truly captive audience.

Commuters have nowhere to go and nothing to do but watch. Your message is seen multiple times within every 30-minute journey slot, ensuring exceptional brand recall and a unique opportunity for direct audience engagement.
